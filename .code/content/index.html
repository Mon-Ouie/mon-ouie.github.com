À propos de Mon ouïe

Mon ouïe is a random nick, which can be found with various spellings depending on the available characters — "Mon Ouïe", "Mon_Ouie", "mon.ouie", and "Mon-Ouie" being some of them.

It usually refers to a mainly Ruby programmer who programs with the sole purpose to program — though some dictonaries claim it refers to one's ability to perceive sounds. It is sometimes said to be mysterious. The "about" page on your left will tell you many things about it, so as to refute this assertion!
